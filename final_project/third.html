Travis Kelce #87

Travis entered his 11th season with the Chiefs in 2023. He has eclipsed the 1,000 yard mark six seasons in a row, making him the first tight end in NFL history to accomplish that feat. He entered the NFL as the first of two third-round picks (63rd overall) by the Kansas City Chiefs in the 2013 NFL Draft.

You can read more about Travis Kelce here: Kansas City Chiefs Official Website

Interesting Facts

  • His big brother, Jason, plays for the Philadelphia Eagles
  • His jersey number is 87 in honor of the year his brother was born
  • In 2016, he had his own reality dating show called Catching Kelce
  • He's currently dating pop-star Taylor Swift

Personal Info

Born: October 5, 1989 Westlake, Ohio

Height: 6 ft 5 in

Weight: 250 lbs

Position: Tight end

High School: Cleveland Heights (Cleveland, Ohio)

College: Cincinnati (2008-2012)

NFL Draft: 2013 / Round: 3 / Pick: 63

Honors

Selected to the Pro Bowl following the 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021 and 2022 seasons.

Received PFWA All-NFL Team honors in 2018 and 2020.

Received PFWA All-AFC Team honors in 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2019.

Received TSN All-NFL Team honors in 2016, 2019, 2020 and 2021.

2020 Chiefs Derrick Thomas Most Valuable Player.

Career NFL Statistics as of 2023

Receptions: 884

Receiving Yards: 11,076

Receiving Touchdowns: 74
