Chris Jones #95

Chris entered his eighth season with the Chiefs in 2023. He set an NFL record in 2018, by recording a sack in 11 consecutive games. He entered the NFL as a second-round pick (37th overall) by the Kansas City Chiefs in the 2016 NFL Draft.

You can read more about Chris Jones here: Kansas City Chiefs Official Website

Interesting Facts

  • Credits his dad for his positive outlook, always being told that he could "always have it worse"
  • Slept on a couch every night after moving in with his grandmother during high school
  • Still attends church with his grandmother every Sunday

Personal Info

Born: July 3, 1994 Houston, Mississippi

Height: 6 ft 6 in

Weight: 310 lbs

Position: Defensive tackle

High School: Houston High School (Houston, Mississippi)

College: Mississippi State (2013-2015)

NFL Draft: 2016 / Round: 2 / Pick: 37

Honors

Selected to the Pro Bowl following the 2019, 2020, 2021 and 2022 seasons.

Received PFWA All-AFC Team honors in 2018, 2019, 2020 and 2021.

Received TSN All-NFL Team honors in 2020 and 2021.

Was voted by teammates as a defensive team captain prior to the club's 2022 playoff games.

2022 AFC Defensive Player of the Year Award.

Career NFL Statistics as of 2023

Tackles: 267

Sacks: 72.5
