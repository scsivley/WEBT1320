Creed Humphrey #52

Creed entered his third season with the Chiefs in 2023. He helped the Chiefs win Super Bowl LVII against the Philadelphia Eagles by anchoring the offensive line that allowed no sacks during the game. He entered the NFL as a second-round pick (63rd overall) by the Kansas City Chiefs in the 2021 NFL Draft.

You can read more about Creed Humphrey here: Kansas City Chiefs Official Website

Interesting Facts

  • He can bench more than 475 pounds and squat more than 700 pounds
  • His previous college teammate, Orlando Brown Jr, was instrumental in Coach Andy Reid's decision to recruit him
  • He didn't allow a single sack during his high school and college career
  • His father was a three time All-American wrestler, who inspired him to start wrestling training at the age of four
