Halloween Spider Cookies

Ingredients:

16 sugar cookies, make your own or use refrigerated cookie dough

16 Reese's Peanut Butter Cup Minis

36 Candy Eyes

1 bag Chocolate Candy Melts

Instructions:

Bake cookies as directed.

As soon as you pull them from the oven, use the bottom of a teaspoon measurer and gently press a divot into the cookie.

Let the cookies cool completely.

Melt the candy melts and place either in a pastry bag or use a zip lock baggie and cut the corner.

Take a Reese’s mini cup and place on cookie using melted chocolate.

Take candy eyes and place on side of Reece’s using melted chocolate.

Use melted chocolate to pipe spider legs on the cookie.

Monster Apples

Ingredients:

green apples, each quartered

sunflower butter

sunflower seeds

strawberries, sliced

Candy Eyes

Instructions:

Cut the middles out of each quarter of the apple to create a mouth.

Coat the inside of the cut with sunflower butter.

Place sunflower seeds on the top of the “mouth” for teeth.

Place 1 sliced strawberry inside the mouth for tongue.

Stick each candy eye above the mouth with sunbutter.

Mummy Hot Dogs

Ingredients:

8 oz. crescent roll sheet (or crescent roll dough)

8 hot dogs

Yellow mustard

Instructions:

Preheat the oven to 375°F.

Open and unroll the crescent roll and cut 1/4 to 1/2-inch wide strips.

Wrap strips around each hotdog.

Spray baking sheet with non-stick spray.

Bake for 12 to 14 minutes, until golden brown.

Let cool before adding two little dots of mustard as eyes.
